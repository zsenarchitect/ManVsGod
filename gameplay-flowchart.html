🎯 Core Mechanics

  • Betting System: Players bet currency on moves
  • Collective Wisdom: God suggests moves based on previous players
  • Disobedience Cost: Pay extra to ignore God's suggestion
  • Dual Scoring: Strategic score + Moral score

🎭 Moral Dilemmas

  • Character Backstories: Rich narratives for each piece
  • Moral Choices: Capture or spare with consequences
  • Philosophical Themes: Utilitarianism vs Deontology
  • Progressive Difficulty: More complex moral situations

📊 Data Collection

  • Player Decisions: Track all choices and timing
  • Google Forms: Real-time data submission
  • Error Logging: Comprehensive error tracking
  • Analytics: Collective wisdom evolution

🎮 Game Flow

  • 10 Progressive Levels: Increasing complexity
  • Random Piece Assignment: Different perspectives each game
  • Real Chess Positions: Famous games and puzzles
  • Mobile Responsive: Play on any device
